My Own Asset Loan

Designed for micro business owners/ entrepreneurs that desire to own a business asset(s) but lack conventional collateral to secure loan from conventional banks.

Features
  • Asset will be insured(comprehensive)
  • Competitive interest rate @ 4%-5%
  • Asset loan of up to N5million
  • Flexible loan tenor up to 24 months
  • Insured loan of 1%.
  • Admin fee of 1%
  • Commitment fee of 1%
  • 10% Equity contribution
Benefits
  • Bonding of different asset
  • Easy documentation
  • Competitive interest rate
  • No hidden charges
Requirements
  • Proforma invoice of asset of choice
  • A/C opening required
  • Prove of business ownership and experience
  • Guarantor
  • Proof of viable business
