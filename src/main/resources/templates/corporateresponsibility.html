Corporate Social Responsibility

Corporate Social Responsibility is very important to us at Accion MfB as we support and empower communities around us. The driving philosophy and objectives of Accion MfB’s corporate social responsibilities is to affect the lives of the under privileged by spurring sustainable economic development in their lives to enable them improve their standard of living.

Our areas of CSR focus range is Education and this is premise on our belief that while empowering the parents to create wealth through our financial empowerment, the generation next should be equipped with solid education that will sustain the wealth.

At Accion MfB we are passionate about education and literacy which is evidenced by our support for various educational projects such as Jakin NGO, AIFA reading society (little writers’ award) and i-volunteer projects. We have also hosted students from various schools on Career Day where they had impacting sessions with our colleagues in various departments of the bank.
