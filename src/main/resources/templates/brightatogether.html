Accion Brighta Togeda

This is a joint account for that for joint savings between two or more persons.

Features
  • Minimum account opening balance N1,000.00
  • Joint account for two or more persons
  • Serves as core savings or target savings account
  • 4.2% interest on savings
Benefits
  • Easy to open
  • Fund can be accessed through cheque, ATM and branch
  • Access to various credit facility
  • Unlimited withdrawals
Requirements
  • Completed account opening form with specimen signature card; and ATM form for signatories of the account
  • Primary or Secondary Means of ID (Not applicable to clients below N200K)
  • Utility bill (Not applicable to clients below N200K)
  • 2 Passport photographs each of the account holders
  • Completed ATM form
